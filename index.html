About the club

The Ithaca Creek State School Swimming Club, known as the Ithaca Sharx, is located in the beautiful grounds of the school. We have a highly professional coaches offering learn-to-swim lessons through to squads, all year round.

We run a family friendly Club Night racing competition every Friday evening during the summer terms. Our focus at club nights is beating your own PB and having fun with your mates.

Contact Us

The heart of the Sharx activities is the Friday club nights during summer, where club swimmers and their families come together for a couple of hours of friendly competition in a positive social environment. We also provide learn to swim (LTS), stroke correction and squad training all year round.

Our club boasts many members from the local community as well. The Ithaca Sharx welcomes new members from the school and wider community at any time.

As a Sub-Committee of the Ithaca Creek State School P&C, the success of the Ithaca Creek Swimming Club relies heavily on the efforts of its volunteers.

We are constantly seeking extra helpers and always need friendly folk to lend a hand. If you have any skills or time you can offer our committee (big or small, any help gratefully received) please get in touch.
